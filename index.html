Property Overview

Architecture in Harmony
with the Sea Ranch Coast

The Glass House sits in an ocean meadow midway along the Sea Ranch coast, its cedar walls and open floor plan an ode to the classic Sea Ranch architectural style. Floor-to-ceiling glass frames a 10-mile stretch of white-water ocean view that changes with every hour of light.

This furnished home sleeps 2–4 guests privately and comfortably across two bedrooms. A double-story living room centers on a sunken inglenook with a stone fireplace, complemented by a loft above and a sheltered view deck with built-in table and benches for outdoor dining.

The kitchen has been newly equipped with Bosch appliances — including a state-of-the-art cooktop and oven, Bosch dishwasher, and GE Profile refrigerator — ready for coastal cooking after a day on the trails.

Just steps from the house, the ocean and walking trails run both north and south along the bluff, with the beach itself only two minutes away.

Community Amenities

Life at Sea Ranch

Sea Ranch offers an unforgettable escape for those who love the outdoors — a rugged coast of trails, headlands, coves, beaches and tide pools, all shared by a close-knit community of just a few thousand seasonal and full-time residents.

Seaside Pool & Sauna

One of three Sea Ranch recreation centers, with a heated pool, sauna, and tennis courts within walking distance.

18-Hole Golf Course

The Robert Muir Graves–designed course winds along the coastal bluffs, open to guests and residents.

Hiking & Biking Trails

Miles of trails run through forest and meadow, with headlands and tide pools to explore in every direction.

Beach & Surf Fishing

The beach is two minutes away on foot, with excellent surf and rock fishing along the coast.

Wildlife Viewing

Deer, fox, rabbit, and mountain quail are common; bird and whale-watching are year-round pastimes.

Gigabit Fiber Internet

High-speed 1GB fiber internet keeps the house connected, even at the edge of the Sonoma Coast.

EV Charging

A Level 2, 240V/40A charging system is available for guests with electric vehicles.

Children’s Playground

A playground and easy trips to Fort Ross and Gualala Point Regional Park round out the family offering.
